# Traffic-Indicators-on-i-94
For this project, I looked at factors that could cause heavy traffic on i-94
I was able to find two types of indicators: Time and Weather Indicators 
- The traffic is usually lighter during the cold month compared to the warmer months
- Traffic is usually heaveir during he day time than at night
- I listed the two tops weather indicators that would cause heavy traffic on i-94:
   Shower snow
   Light rain and snow
